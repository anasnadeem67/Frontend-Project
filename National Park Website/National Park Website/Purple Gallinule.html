Purple Gallinules Information



Habbit

Purple Gallinules are almost always found near freshwater, even during migration. For nesting, they favor freshwater ponds with emergent vegetation, and the species has adapted well to many human-altered environments.



Diet

Omnivorous. Eats a wide variety of plant and animal matter, including seeds, fruits, and leaves of aquatic and terrestrial plants, also insects, frogs, snails, spiders, worms, fish. At times, eats the eggs and young of other birds.
